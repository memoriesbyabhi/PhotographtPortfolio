

# Memories by Abhi — Photography Portfolio

## Overview
A stunning, professionally designed single-page photography portfolio with rich Indian traditional aesthetics (70% Indian / 30% American), smooth animations, embedded video content, and a WhatsApp contact CTA. Tagline: *"Capturing Timeless Indian Traditions"*.

## Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Deep burgundy/maroon, gold accents, cream/ivory backgrounds — inspired by Indian wedding aesthetics
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body text
- **Decorative elements**: Subtle mandala patterns, paisley borders, gold ornamental dividers
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-ins, parallax effects, smooth section transitions
